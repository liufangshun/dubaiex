PRIVACY STATEMENT

This Privacy Statement explains how X Blockchain Pty Ltd ACN 621 727 795 (we, us, and our) collects, holds, uses and discloses your personal information.

Personal information is information or an opinion about an identified, or reasonably identifiable, individual. This may include your name, date of birth, residential address, email address, bank account details, and the details on your passport, drivers licence, Medicare card, social security card or national identity card.

Providing information about another person

When you give us personal information about another person, you represent that you are authorised to do so and agree to inform the person that we have collected their information as part of this application, and of the contents of this Privacy Statement.

Purposes for collecting personal information

We will generally collect your personal information directly from you, however in certain circumstances we may collect your information from third parties and/or publically available information sources.

We collect your personal information in order to provide you with digital currency trading services, and in particular for the following purposes:

⋅ to assess and process your application for an account with us, including to identify you;

⋅ to open your account;

⋅ to facilitate and administer your access to, and use of, your account and our services;

⋅ to execute your instructions and process your trades;

⋅ to communicate with you, including about the status of your account and any trade;

⋅ to process payments or transfers of digital currency between you and other users of our services;

⋅ to manage our relationship with you (including account management, support and handling enquiries and complaints);

⋅ to investigate and take steps to prevent illegal or prohibited activities;

⋅ to comply with legislative or regulatory requirements;

for any internal processes including product development and service improvement, strategic planning, risk management, fraud prevention and pricing;

⋅ to meet our obligations in relation to government bodies and external payments; and

⋅ to identify and (unless you tell us not to) contact you to discuss other products and services that may be of interest to you.

Without your personal information, we may be unable to provide you with our services.

Persons to whom your personal information is disclosed

We may disclose your personal information to:

⋅ our related bodies corporate, assignees, agents, contractors and external advisers;

⋅ our service providers, including organisations for verifying your identity and providing fraud detection services;

⋅ your agents, advisers, executors, administrators and attorneys;

⋅ any person we consider necessary to execute your instructions;

⋅ any person who is considering whether to acquire or who has acquired any part of our business, or the rights or obligations under the contract that we have with you; and

⋅ law enforcement, regulatory and government bodies.

We may disclose your personal information to overseas recipients who provide services and products to us and for administrative, data storage or other business management purposes. It is not practicable to list all of the countries in which these recipients will be located, but it is likely that they will include Canada, China, Hong Kong, India, Japan, Indonesia, Singapore, the Philippines, Russia, the United Kingdom and the United States of America.

Your rights

You have the right to request access and correction of the personal information that we hold about you. You also have the right to make a complaint about the way in which we manage your personal information.
